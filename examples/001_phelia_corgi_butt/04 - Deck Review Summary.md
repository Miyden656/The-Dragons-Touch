The guided review was run as an optional tuning pass for a legal 100-card Commander deck.

Because the deck was already legal, the tool did not treat any cuts as mandatory. Instead, it reviewed possible optimization cuts and collection-only replacement options.

- The tool generated a full deck report with the complete decklist
- The report included annotated card role notes
- The guided prompt asked for the deck report before beginning the review
- The review moved section by section
- The review accepted numbered answers
- The user could correct the tool’s strategy read
- The review respected a Theme / Vibe philosophy lens
- The review respected the Bethany, The Theme Mentor guide/persona
- The review kept recommendations within collection-only and $0 outside purchases
- The review produced optional cuts instead of treating a legal deck as needing mandatory cuts
- The final output included suggested swaps, protected cards, and playtest questions
