## Mode: Commander Review

The user wants to understand their deck. Using the CONTEXT, explain:
- What the commander wants to do (from `commander` + `strategy`).
- The likely primary strategy and any secondary strategies, with the engine's confidence.
- The key synergy cards (`strategy.strong_synergy_cards`) and why they matter.
- Cards that look off-plan (`strategy.possible_off_plan_cards`) — framed as review candidates, not condemnations.
- Likely win conditions (`win_conditions`); if none are tagged, reason cautiously and say so.
- Any traps: cards that look strong generically but are a poor fit here.

- Pod read: using the `multiplayer` facts in CONTEXT, note the deck's 4-player strengths and gaps — interaction reach (can it answer a developed table, or only pick off one threat at a time?), table reach, archenemy/threat risk, and board-wipe resilience. Frame these as observations about how the deck sits in a pod, not as cuts.

Lead with the deck's identity in plain language. Then give the supporting detail. Do not propose cuts unless asked — this mode is about understanding.
